How to Make a Mask


Masks are one of the most important tools to fight against COVID-19, as it protects others and you. To make your mask effective, make sure that it covers your nose to chin and fits comfortably around the sides without any gaps. You need to wear a mask if: you are above two years old, any form of transportation or transportation hub or when there are guests.There are three most common masks to wear: cloth masks, surgical masks and N95 masks.


Cloth Masks

A cloth mask should be made of tightly wooven fabrics (such as cotton and cotton blends), is breathable and is of at least two or three fabric layers for it to be effective.
